Eligibility Requirements

Do You Qualify for the MSFH Grant?

Most Florida homeowners qualify. Here are the requirements for the 2026 cycle.

🏠

Primary Residence

Must be your homesteaded Florida property — not a rental or vacation home.

💰

Insured Value ≤ $700K

Your dwelling Coverage A must not exceed $700,000. Low-income homeowners are exempt from this requirement.

📋

Active Insurance

You must have an active homeowners insurance policy — any Florida-admitted carrier qualifies.

⭐

Priority Groups

Low-income (≤80% AMI) get first access, no match required. Moderate-income (80-120% AMI) follow. Apply in your group window.
