Hurricane Ian's devastating impact in September 2022 demonstrated exactly why these counties need maximum protection—wind speeds exceeded 150 mph in some areas, causing billions in property damage.

My Safe Florida Home Grant: Up to $10,000 for Lee and Collier County Residents

Florida homeowners in Lee and Collier Counties may qualify for the My Safe Florida Home grant program, which provides up to $10,000 toward the cost of hurricane mitigation improvements, including impact windows. This state-funded program, established under Florida Statute 215.5586, helps offset the initial investment while you immediately begin receiving insurance savings.

Grant Opportunity: The My Safe Florida Home program offers Lee and Collier County homeowners up to $10,000 in matching funds for impact window installation. Combined with insurance savings averaging $3,036 per year, most homeowners see complete return on investment within 5-7 years.

How the Grant Program Works

Qualifying for the My Safe Florida Home grant requires meeting specific criteria established by the Florida Department of Financial Services. Your home must be owner-occupied, built before 2009, and have an insured value under $700,000. A free wind mitigation inspection determines your eligibility and identifies which improvements will provide the greatest benefit.
