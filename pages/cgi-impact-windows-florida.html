CGI Impact Windows in Florida: Premium Hurricane Protection Solutions

When it comes to protecting your Florida home from devastating hurricanes and tropical storms, CGI impact windows stand as one of the industry's most trusted solutions. As a leading manufacturer of hurricane-resistant fenestration products, CGI Windows and Doors has earned its reputation through rigorous testing, innovative engineering, and unwavering commitment to quality. Florida homeowners seeking the ultimate protection against storm damage consistently turn to CGI's comprehensive product lines for their superior performance and lasting value.

At Florida Impact Shield, we specialize in installing CGI impact windows in Florida, helping property owners throughout the state safeguard their investments while enhancing energy efficiency and property values. Our expert team understands the unique challenges Florida's climate presents and can guide you through selecting the perfect CGI window system for your specific needs.

Why Choose CGI Impact Windows for Florida Homes

CGI Windows and Doors has manufactured impact-resistant products for over 30 years, establishing themselves as an industry pioneer in impact window technology. Their Florida-based manufacturing facilities produce windows specifically engineered to withstand the state's harshest weather conditions, from Category 5 hurricanes to daily exposure to intense UV radiation and salt air corrosion.

The company's commitment to excellence extends beyond basic hurricane protection. Every CGI impact window undergoes extensive testing to meet or exceed the strictest building codes in Florida, including Miami-Dade County's notoriously rigorous requirements. This dedication ensures that homeowners receive products that deliver multi-layered benefits including forced entry protection, noise reduction, UV filtration, and exceptional energy efficiency.

Miami-Dade NOA Certification: The Gold Standard

CGI impact windows carry Miami-Dade County Notice of Acceptance (NOA) certification, the most stringent hurricane protection standard in the United States. This certification requires products to withstand large missile impact tests, cyclic pressure testing simulating hurricane-force winds, and water penetration resistance—ensuring your windows will perform when you need them most.

CGI Impact Window Product Lines

CGI offers three distinct product series, each designed to address specific performance requirements and architectural preferences. Understanding the differences between these CGI impact window lines helps Florida homeowners make informed decisions about their hurricane protection investments.

CGI Sentinel Series Impact Windows

The CGI Sentinel Series represents the company's flagship line of impact-resistant windows, combining advanced engineering with architectural versatility. This series features:

  • Heavy-duty aluminum construction with multi-chamber reinforcement for maximum structural integrity
  • Advanced laminated glass technology that maintains transparency while stopping windborne debris
  • Multiple configuration options including single-hung, horizontal roller, fixed, and casement styles
  • Coastal-grade finishes that resist corrosion from salt air and humidity
  • Energy-efficient glazing options that reduce cooling costs in Florida's intense heat
  • Miami-Dade NOA certification for the highest level of hurricane protection
  • Customizable colors and hardware to complement any architectural style

The Sentinel Series excels in both residential and commercial applications, offering design flexibility without compromising on protection. These windows integrate seamlessly into new construction projects or replacement window installations throughout Florida.

CGI StormCalm Impact Windows

For homeowners seeking premium aesthetics combined with uncompromising protection, the CGI StormCalm Series delivers refined elegance. This product line emphasizes:

  • Sleeker profiles and narrower sightlines for maximum glass area and enhanced views
  • Advanced weatherstripping systems that create superior seals against wind and water
  • Smooth, whisper-quiet operation even after years of use in harsh Florida conditions
  • Enhanced acoustic insulation that dramatically reduces exterior noise pollution
  • Premium hardware options including multi-point locking systems
  • Color-matched components for a cohesive, high-end appearance
  • Full Miami-Dade County approval for ultimate peace of mind

The StormCalm Series particularly appeals to luxury homeowners and those in upscale communities where architectural aesthetics matter as much as hurricane protection. These windows prove that safety and beauty aren't mutually exclusive.

CGI Thermally Broken Impact Windows

Florida's climate demands more than just hurricane protection—energy efficiency directly impacts comfort and utility costs. The CGI thermally broken series addresses this need through innovative thermal barrier technology:

  • Polyamide thermal breaks that interrupt heat transfer between interior and exterior aluminum components
  • Significantly improved U-factors and energy performance ratings
  • Reduced condensation formation even in highly air-conditioned Florida interiors
  • Enhanced comfort through elimination of hot or cold window surfaces
  • Qualification for energy-related rebates and incentives
  • Proven impact resistance with superior thermal performance
  • Ideal for high-performance home designs and green building projects

The thermally broken series represents CGI's commitment to evolving beyond basic code compliance, delivering windows that contribute to sustainable, energy-conscious construction while maintaining the hurricane protection Florida demands.

Frequently Asked Questions About CGI Impact Windows

▼

Are CGI impact windows worth the investment for Florida homeowners?

Absolutely. CGI impact windows provide multi-layered value including hurricane protection, insurance discounts, energy savings, noise reduction, UV protection, and increased property values. Most Florida homeowners recoup their investment through insurance savings and reduced energy costs within 7-10 years, while enjoying immediate peace of mind.

▼

What makes Miami-Dade NOA certification so important?

Miami-Dade County maintains the nation's strictest building codes due to its hurricane vulnerability. NOA certification requires products to pass large missile impact tests (simulating debris traveling at 50 feet per second), cyclic pressure testing equivalent to Category 5 hurricane winds, and rigorous water resistance testing. Products bearing this certification meet or exceed requirements throughout Florida and other hurricane-prone regions.

▼

How do CGI thermally broken windows improve energy efficiency?

Thermally broken windows incorporate a non-conductive polyamide barrier between interior and exterior aluminum components, interrupting thermal transfer. This technology prevents outdoor heat from conducting through window frames into air-conditioned interiors, reducing cooling loads and improving comfort while maintaining full impact resistance.

▼

Can I replace just a few windows with CGI impact windows or should I do the whole house?

While you can replace windows individually, completing your entire home offers significant advantages. Full protection eliminates vulnerable points during hurricanes, maximizes insurance discounts (many carriers require all openings protected for full credits), and ensures consistent appearance and performance throughout your property.

▼

How long do CGI impact windows last in Florida's harsh climate?

CGI impact windows are engineered specifically for Florida's demanding conditions including intense UV exposure, high humidity, salt air, and temperature extremes. With proper maintenance, these windows typically perform optimally for 20-30 years or more. CGI backs their products with comprehensive warranties, demonstrating confidence in long-term durability.
