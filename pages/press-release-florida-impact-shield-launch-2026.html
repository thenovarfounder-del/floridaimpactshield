# Florida Impact Shield Launches Comprehensive Digital Platform to Serve All 67 Florida Counties with Hurricane Protection Solutions

## Statewide Expansion Features AI-Powered Instant Quotes, My Safe Florida Home Grant Assistance, and Insurance Savings Up to 45%

**Miami, FL — June 15, 2026** — Florida Impact Shield, a leading hurricane protection contractor and My Safe Florida Home approved provider, today announced the launch of its comprehensive digital platform designed to connect Florida homeowners across all 67 counties with certified impact window and door installation services, insurance discount assistance, and state grant programs.

The new platform addresses the growing demand for hurricane protection solutions statewide by offering homeowners instant access to licensed contractors, real-time quotes through AI technology, and comprehensive resources for accessing available insurance savings and state grants of up to $10,000 through the My Safe Florida Home program.

"Florida homeowners deserve protection from hurricanes and access to every dollar of savings available to them," said Michael Richardson, CEO of Florida Impact Shield. "Our new digital platform removes the barriers that have traditionally made hurricane hardening expensive and complicated. Whether you're in Pensacola or Key West, homeowners can now get instant quotes, understand their insurance savings potential, and connect with certified contractors who can help them access state grants—all in one place."

Florida Impact Shield's platform offers a complete suite of hurricane protection services including impact-resistant windows, impact doors, sliding glass door systems, and hurricane-rated garage doors. As a My Safe Florida Home approved contractor with licensed CBC (Certified Building Contractor) status, the company is uniquely positioned to help homeowners navigate both the installation process and grant application requirements.

The platform's standout feature is Eva, an AI-powered chatbot that provides instant preliminary quotes based on homeowners' specific needs. By answering a few simple questions about their property, Florida residents can receive immediate pricing information and learn about potential insurance discounts and grant eligibility without waiting for a sales call or in-home estimate.

Homeowners who upgrade to impact-rated windows and doors can reduce their annual insurance premiums by up to 45% through wind mitigation discounts. When combined with My Safe Florida Home grants of up to $10,000, the out-of-pocket costs for hurricane protection can be dramatically reduced, making these life-saving upgrades accessible to more Florida families than ever before.

"We've seen insurance premiums skyrocket across Florida, but many homeowners don't realize that impact windows and proper wind mitigation can cut those costs nearly in half," Richardson added. "Add in the state grants, and for many families, hurricane protection becomes not just affordable—it becomes the smart financial choice."

Florida Impact Shield has earned a 4.9 out of 5.0 customer rating based on hundreds of installations completed since the company began serving Florida in 2018. The company's CBC license ensures compliance with Florida's stringent building codes and contractor requirements, giving homeowners confidence in both the quality of installation and eligibility for insurance discounts.

The statewide expansion comes as Florida continues to face increasing hurricane threats and as insurance companies tighten requirements for coverage. The My Safe Florida Home program has allocated millions in funding to help Florida residents strengthen their homes against storm damage, but many homeowners remain unaware of the program or find the application process daunting.

Florida Impact Shield's platform streamlines the entire process from initial quote through installation and insurance documentation. The company's network of certified installers ensures consistent quality and compliance across all 67 counties, from the Panhandle to South Florida.

Homeowners can access the platform 24/7 to explore options, chat with Eva for instant quotes, schedule free inspections, and learn about maximizing their insurance savings and grant opportunities.
