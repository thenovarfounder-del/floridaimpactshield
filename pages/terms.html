2. Services Description

Florida Impact Shield specializes in residential and commercial hurricane protection services throughout the State of Florida. Our core services include:

2.1 Impact Window Installation

We provide professional installation of hurricane impact-resistant windows designed to meet or exceed Florida Building Code requirements and Miami-Dade County protocols where applicable. Our impact windows are engineered to withstand high winds and flying debris during severe weather events.

2.2 Impact Door Installation

We install hurricane impact-resistant doors, including entry doors, sliding glass doors, and French doors, all compliant with applicable Florida Building Code standards and designed to provide maximum protection during hurricane conditions.

2.3 Wind Mitigation Inspections

We offer professional wind mitigation inspection services conducted by licensed inspectors. These inspections evaluate your property's wind-resistant features and generate official reports (Form OIR-B1-1802) that may qualify you for homeowners insurance discounts.

2.4 Grant Application Assistance

We provide assistance with applications for the My Safe Florida Home (MSFH) grant program and other applicable hurricane mitigation grant programs. Our services include guidance through the application process, documentation preparation, and compliance verification. We do not guarantee grant approval or funding amounts.

2.5 Additional Services

Additional services may be offered at our discretion and are subject to separate agreement terms when applicable. All services are performed in accordance with applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations, and building codes.

7. My Safe Florida Home Grant Program Terms

7.1 Program Overview

The My Safe Florida Home (MSFH) grant program is a state-funded initiative administered by the Florida Department of Financial Services designed to help Florida homeowners strengthen their homes against hurricanes. Florida Impact Shield provides assistance with applications for this program as a service to our customers.

7.2 Eligibility Requirements

Grant eligibility is determined solely by the State of Florida and is subject to program requirements, which may include but are not limited to:

  • Property must be a site-built, single-family residential home
  • Home must be the primary residence of the applicant
  • Home must have a insured value within program limits
  • Homeowner must meet income requirements if applicable
  • Home must have been built before the current Florida Building Code adoption
  • Homeowner must maintain homeowners insurance
  • Program funds must be available at time of application

Program requirements and availability are subject to change by the State of Florida without notice.

7.3 Our Role

Florida Impact Shield's grant assistance services include:

  • Providing general information about program eligibility and benefits
  • Assisting with completion of application forms
  • Helping gather required documentation
  • Coordinating wind mitigation inspections if required
  • Providing estimates and specifications that meet program requirements
  • Facilitating communication with program administrators
  • Ensuring installed products meet program compliance standards

7.4 No Guarantee of Approval

FLORIDA IMPACT SHIELD DOES NOT GUARANTEE THAT YOUR GRANT APPLICATION WILL BE APPROVED OR THAT YOU WILL RECEIVE ANY SPECIFIC AMOUNT OF FUNDING. Grant approval and funding amounts are determined entirely by the State of Florida based on program criteria, available funding, application completeness, and other factors beyond our control. Our assistance with the application process does not constitute any representation or warranty regarding approval or funding.

7.5 Application Fees

Any fees charged by the state or third parties for grant applications, inspections, or processing are the sole responsibility of the Customer. Florida Impact Shield's assistance with the grant application process is provided at no additional charge to customers who contract for installation services with us.

7.6 Grant Fund Timing

Grant funds, if approved, are typically disbursed according to the program's procedures, which may include reimbursement after work completion or direct payment to contractors. Customers are responsible for understanding the grant payment process. In most cases, customers must pay for services upfront and then receive reimbursement from the grant program, or grant funds may be paid directly to Florida Impact Shield upon project completion and submission of required documentation.

7.7 Customer Responsibilities

When applying for grant assistance, you are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate and complete information on all applications
  • Meeting all application deadlines
  • Responding to requests for additional information from program administrators
  • Maintaining eligibility throughout the application and installation process
  • Complying with all program requirements and restrictions
  • Understanding that grant denial does not void your contract for services

7.8 Program Changes

The My Safe Florida Home grant program is subject to funding availability, legislative changes, and administrative modifications. The program may be suspended, modified, or terminated at any time by the State of Florida. Florida Impact Shield is not responsible for program changes that affect your eligibility or funding amount.

7.9 Contract Independence

Your contract with Florida Impact Shield for installation services is independent of grant approval. If your grant application is denied or if you receive less funding than anticipated, you remain obligated to fulfill the payment terms of your contract with us. We recommend not signing a contract if grant funding is essential to your ability to pay unless you have received written grant approval.
