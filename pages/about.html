Our Story

Florida-born. Hurricane-tested. Insurance-smart.

Florida Impact Shield was founded by Florida natives who watched neighbors scramble with plywood every storm season — and then get crushed by rising insurance premiums. We built this company to solve both problems permanently.

We are a Florida Certified Building Contractor (License: CBC1265XXX), approved by the My Safe Florida Home program, and certified to install Miami-Dade NOA-approved products in HVHZ zones. Our crews are licensed, insured, and background-checked.

We specialize exclusively in hurricane impact windows and doors — it's all we do, and we do it better than anyone in Florida.
