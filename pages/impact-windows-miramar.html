HVHZ Compliance for Broward County Properties

Miramar's location within Broward County's High Velocity Hurricane Zone mandates specific building code requirements for all window installations. Impact windows must undergo rigorous testing protocols including:

  • Large missile impact testing simulating windborne debris
  • Small missile impact resistance from gravel and smaller projectiles
  • Cyclic pressure testing exceeding 180 mph wind equivalents
  • Water infiltration resistance under extreme conditions
  • Miami-Dade County Notice of Acceptance (NOA) certification
  • Florida Product Approval verification

Our impact window systems exceed these demanding standards, providing Riviera Isles residents and homeowners throughout Miramar with complete confidence that their properties meet all local building department requirements for permitting and inspections.
