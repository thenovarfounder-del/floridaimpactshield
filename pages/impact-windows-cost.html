By Window Type

Price by Window Style

Costs vary by product type, size, and installation complexity.

🪟

Single-Hung

$700–950
Per opening installed. Most common for bedrooms and secondary rooms. Simple mechanics, excellent wind performance.
🏠

Casement / Awning

$950–1,300
Per opening installed. Premium seal when closed. Popular in coastal areas for maximum protection and cleaner sight lines.
🚪

Sliding Glass Doors

$2,200–4,500
Per door unit installed. Large panels require heavy-duty hardware. Available in 6-ft, 8-ft, and 10-ft widths.
🏙️

Picture / Fixed

$600–900
Per opening. No moving parts — purely structural. Often used alongside operable windows for large wall areas.
🚿

Horizontal Roller

$800–1,100
Per opening. Smooth operation, popular in living areas. Easy to clean from inside.
🚪

Entry / French Doors

$2,800–5,500
Per door unit installed. Full impact frame and glass. Adds curb appeal while meeting HVHZ code requirements.
